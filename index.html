Saving. Healing. Loving.
Cats.

At Purrfect Match Cat Rescue, we help homeless kitties on their journeys find forever homes.

We take in stray, owner-surrendered, or shelter cats and provide them with proper veterinary care. Then, in our warm, comfortable facility that we call MeowHouse, we provide cats with safe housing and socialization. Finally, we partner with trustworthy adoption agencies near and far so that our cats—and their humans—can find their purrfect match!

Since 2015, we have saved over 3,000 cats.

As a non-profit, we function because of the generous time of our volunteers and financial support of our contributors. Would you consider partnering with us today?
